Interests

Roller Coasters I have always been interested in theme parks and roller coasters. I have gone to an extensive number of amusement and theme parks around the country. Check out my Roller Coaster Journel to see which parks and which roller coasters I have ridden.

Astronomy The cosmos, the stars, and the constellations are an interest of mine. Serving as Astronomy Club President for two terms, I was able to have access the Oakley Observatory at Rose-Hulman and use the telescopes there. I am not president anymore, but I occasionally enjoy looking at the night sky.

Running 5k's I try to exercise and work out to keep in shape as well as for entertainment. If there is a local 5k in town, I will race in it. I am not the best runner, but I do run.
